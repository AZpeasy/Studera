Making Students Powerful Everyday.

At Studera, we believe the greatest power is opportunity.

Yet today, most students receive generic college counselling. A typical school counsellor works with between 35 to 70 students from the same school. Everyone gets the same vague personal statement and essay edits, the same university suggestions based only on grades, and the same general scholarship lists. This advice does not help a student reach their dream university.

Studera changes this. We connect high school students with current undergraduate students from their exact target university and programme. These mentors, who share the same country of origin, schooling system, and come from a similar background, can tell you exactly what the university looks for in applicants and help you construct and edit your essays accordingly. They can guide you on which extracurriculars to pursue, as well as financial aid and scholarship opportunities tied to your major. Most importantly, they can evaluate your university selection on more than just your grades, sharing first-hand experience to help you decide whether that university really is for you.

They can give you resources that no generic counsellor even knows about. The guidance transforms from vague and generic to context-friendly and effective. This is how we make students powerful everyday. We give students an exact path to their dream university from someone who has already walked it.
